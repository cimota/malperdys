The Dukes and Princes of Mongo, have domain over the various lands of the amalgam planet of [[Nibiru]].

The Forest Kingdom of [[Arboria]] is ruled by [[Prince Barin]].
The Ice Kingdom of Frigia is ruled by [[Queen Frigia]], of the [[Giants]]
The Jungle Kingdom of [[Tropica]] is ruled by [[Queen Desira]]
The Undersea Kingdom of [[Shar]] is ruled by [[King Kala]]
The [[Flying City]] of the Hawkmen is ruled by [[Prince Vultan]]
The [[Land of the Plains]] is ruled by [[Prince Thun]] of the Lion Men.

On pain of death, have all sworn fealty to the [[Ming|ruler]] of Nibiru.
