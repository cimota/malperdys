Sarnash is an immortal ruler of the [[Serpent People]], who has lived in [[Hyperborea]] for millennia.

Sarnash is a **regression**, having humanoid upper limbs and a serpentine lower half (giving them a lamia-like appearance. Like most [[Serpent People]], Sarnash can present as male or female over their life, responding to environment and the influence of [[Magic|magic]].
