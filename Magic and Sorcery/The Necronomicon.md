An [[Ontonic Objects|Ontonic Object]], The Book contains forbidden wisdom and ultimately the doom of the researcher.

In some versions, the book contains the names of the dead, and only the dead. Trying to look up your own name will reveal the date of your death. It is a useful reference for anyone doing research.
