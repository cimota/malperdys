The Bloom is believed to be the inciting event that created the [[Shift]] and the first [[Brane]]. Some [[Irisian]] lore claims that this first [[Brane]] was the original home-universe of their race. But as time within the [[Brane|Branes]] is relative and local, this is impossible to verify.
